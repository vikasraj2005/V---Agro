About Us

About V Agro Overseas

Welcome to V Agro Overseas, a trusted exporter and supplier of premium-quality agricultural products from India. We specialize in sourcing, grading, packaging, and exporting fresh onions, potatoes, and garlic to global markets.

With a strong network of experienced farmers, modern packaging facilities, and reliable logistics partners, we ensure every shipment meets international quality standards while preserving freshness throughout transportation.

Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and timely delivery has helped us build long-lasting relationships with importers, wholesalers, supermarkets, and distributors across the world.

Mission

Deliver high-quality agricultural products while building lasting relationships based on trust and reliability.

Vision

Become one of India's most trusted agricultural export companies.

Values

Quality, integrity, reliability and customer satisfaction guide every shipment.
